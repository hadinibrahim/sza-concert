About the Concert

After a series of sold-out shows around the world, SZA is bringing her 2025 tour to Japan for a special one-night performance in Saitama Super Arena. Expect a night filled with genre-bending R&B, ethereal visuals, and raw emotion. Whether you’re a longtime fan or new to her music, this concert promises an unforgettable experience

SZA’s 2025 Japan concert offers five ticket tiers at Saitama Super Arena:

  • VIP Front Row – ¥35,000: Closest to the stage with early entry and exclusive merch.
  • Premium Floor – ¥25,000: Amazing floor view just behind VIP.
  • Stadium A – ¥18,000: Great 200-level seats with clear stage view.
  • Stadium B – ¥13,000: Side/rear 200-level seats with solid views.
  • Balcony – ¥9,000–¥11,000: Upper-level seats with a full arena view.

    • All seats are reserved. Tickets go on sale August 15, 2025 at https://www.livenation.co.jp
